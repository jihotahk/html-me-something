Leicester City FC - Does the Data Show Their Success?

By Jiho Tahk

Soccer > Data Analysis

In the 2015-2016 season of the English Premier League, Leicester City FC were expected by most experts to battle to stay in top-flight soccer. A soocer club in a town roughly the size of Wichita, Kansas, Leicester were 5000 - 1 to win the Premier League. In one of the most outlandish underdog story in soccer, if not all of sports, Leicester City lifted the Premier League trophy for the first time in its 132 year history. How did they accomplish this feat? Let's see if the data reveals anything.

This report will document the thought process and initial findings of the exploratory analysis of the 2016 English Premier League data obtained from OPTA stats. The analysis focuses on Leicester City in the 2015-2016 season, a team that miraculously defied the odds and all convention to win the title. I will try to determine if there are any clues in the data that may explain the success of a team most believe would be fighting off relegation.
